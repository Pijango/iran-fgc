About Our Project

This Is CZ Exhaust

We started our research based on the 6 Project Sectors we had and after several brainstorming sessions we decided to choose the Transport Sector. According to our research, since 2010, more than 60% of Tehranâ€™s air pollution is generated from transportation . This is caused due to low quality fuel, low quality cars, poor road and urban planning. We also need to consider the fact that the technology of electric cars is not going to be applied in our country in the near future.

Step One

There is a chamber with NaOH inside our exhaust, where gas enters through one side and dissolves the CO2, transforming it into sodium carbonate precipitation.

Step Two

There is an electrostatic charging via electrodes to particles and then through the way particles are moving, they end up being absorbed into the filters inside the device.

Step Three

Passing through a magnetic field and moving in a circular path where they end up sticking to the filters that are placed inside.

Step Four

Electrical charged particles are dangerous and may stick to human lungs, therefore, after step three, all remaining particles are processed through HEPA filters.

Step Five

Finally, the smoke passes from containing a graphene nanoparticle metal foam box, which is placed inside in the trunk of the car and It is possible to replace the box and foams inside the car trunk after a specific period of time.


Conclusion

The Problem is Filters capacity and Replacement of used filters. And the Solution is Using a mechanical exchange system with a sensor to automatically exchange filters and passing through the NaOH solution. We will continue to do so and fully develop the process to achieve our main goals, even after First Global. We are in the process of researching the conversion of captured carbon into Biochar, which is a soil conditioner and stable storage of carbon.
