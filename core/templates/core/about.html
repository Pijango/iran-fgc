About Our Team

This Is Team Iran

In 2017, this team entered the first round of this challenge as the only representative from Iran. Furthermore, it won the 14th place in the competition among other 157 countries.
Next years the team participated in the 2018 Mexico and 2019 Dubai competitions. Moreover, the tournament organizers gave it special attention in all these periods.

In 2020, in the fourth edition of this World Olympiad, the Iranian team, with the presence of 35 students, succeeded in qualifying to be in the Top 20 teams with two projects: an intelligent system for managing and saving water consumption and a device for disinfecting clothes and equipment , and also along with eight other countries.
Our mentor, Mohammadreza Karami, also won the Outstanding Mentor Award

The fourth and fifth rounds of the First Global World Olympiad were held online in the last two years due to the Coronavirus. The Iranian team participated in the fifth round of the competition in 2021 with the presence of 16 students from all over the country, and by presenting Smart trash can for compost production, it won 2nd place in the FIRST Global Grand Challenge Award in the Environment branch, which was a source of pride for the country of Iran.

This team, which has been continuously invited to all competitions by the managers of the first global since the first round of the competition, this year, after the official invitation, is busy with planning and preparations to participate in the sixth round of the competition in Switzerland.
